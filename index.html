Our YFU App!

YFU provides a student app to help you keep up to date with everything while you are studying abroad. You can contact other students in your area, keep on-top of your study schedule and find out updates and information directly from YFU throughout your trip. You can feel secure in knowing that all the information you need is right at your fingertips!

Join Our
Photo Challenge

We have challenged our current students who are following their dreams abroad to create an interesting and inspiring photo journal so that their family, friends and fellow students can follow them and see how they are living their life abroad. These journals also give insight to fellow students so they can see what is in store for them.

Info Night

YFU frequently has information nights in many cities around Denmark where we offer you information about the programmes we offer, what to expect and the opportunity to meet with previous students and discuss their experiences. We know that you have a lot of questions and our info night will have you leaving with all your quesions answered!
